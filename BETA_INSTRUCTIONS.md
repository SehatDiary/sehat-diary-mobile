# Sehat Diary — Beta Testing Instructions

## Installation (Android)

1. Download the APK file shared with you via WhatsApp/link
2. Open the file on your Android phone
3. If prompted, tap **"Install anyway"** (the app is not on Play Store yet)
4. If asked to allow installs from unknown sources, go to **Settings > Security > Allow from this source**
5. Once installed, open **Sehat Diary**

## Getting Started

### For Caregivers (देखभालकर्ता)

1. Enter your **10-digit phone number** and tap **OTP भेजें**
2. Enter the **6-digit OTP** received via SMS
3. You will see the **Family Dashboard**
4. Tap **"परिवार का सदस्य जोड़ें"** to add a family member
5. Tap a family member to see their health sessions
6. Tap **"नया दौरा"** to start a new health session
7. In a session, tap the **📋 +** button to upload a prescription photo
8. Review the extracted medicines and tap **"दवाइयाँ पुष्टि करें"**

### For Patients (मरीज़)

1. Login with your phone number and OTP
2. You will see **"आज की दवाइयाँ"** (Today's Medicines)
3. Medicines are grouped by time: सुबह, दोपहर, शाम, रात
4. Tap **"ली ✓"** when you take a medicine
5. The progress ring at the top shows how many medicines you've taken today
